Who We Are

At Orange Coast Concierge, we provide a comprehensive range of physical therapy services designed to prevent future falls and injuries. Our offerings include Stretch and Strengthening programs, Aquatics therapy, and Pilates, which focus on improving flexibility, balance, and core strength. Additionally, we specialize in Myofascial Release (MFR) and Massage therapy to alleviate tension and enhance mobility. For our younger clients, we deliver Pediatric therapy, while Occupational Therapy and Speech Language Pathology services are available to support various rehabilitation needs. To accommodate different lifestyles, we also provide Telehealth options and the convenience of in-home or in-office assistance, ensuring that all individuals receive tailored care that promotes safety and wellness.
